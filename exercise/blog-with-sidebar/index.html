SHOPS LUXERIE

HAIR

by C'irsten Walker ~ January 31, 2025

Every human being at least experienced getting their hair done once, whether it was a style or a cut. Barbershops are the most common places to provide haircuts, while beauty shops are the most common places for women to receive services for their hair. These two establishments are places providing needed community bonds, social interactions and many other connections.

Beauty Shop

Beauty shops are most common for women to come and receive services for their hair. To many this is a place of freedom and innocence. Women take the day to be beautified, relaxed and have a good time with conversation.

Barber Shop

Barber shops are typically where men receive haircuts. Just like beauty shops are safe places for women; barber shops are the same for men. The timing is different in barber shops than beauty shops. It can take women hours to get their hair the correct way, but for men all the time needed is a maximum of 30 minutes.
